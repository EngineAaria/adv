GST Knowledge Mini-Guide

A simple guide covering common GST questions. Designed for clients and new staff to understand basics without confusion.

What is GST?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is a single indirect tax on supply of goods and services across India. It replaces multiple earlier taxes.

What is GST Registration?

GST Registration is mandatory once turnover crosses the prescribed limit or when specific conditions apply. Without registration, GST cannot be charged.

What is GSTR-1?

GSTR-1 is a return showing outward supplies (sales). It contains invoice-wise details of sales made during the period.

What is GSTR-3B?

GSTR-3B is a summary return where tax liability is declared and paid. It does not contain invoice-level details.

What is ITC (Input Tax Credit)?

ITC means claiming credit of GST paid on purchases against GST payable on sales. ITC is allowed only when specific legal conditions are satisfied.

What is RCM?

Under Reverse Charge Mechanism, the recipient pays GST instead of the supplier. It applies only to specified goods, services, or unregistered suppliers.

What happens if GST returns are late?

Late filing attracts late fees and interest. Continuous delay can lead to notices and blocking of ITC.
